Our Story

Brewing with Purpose

The Artisanal Hearth was founded by Vikram Nair, a former software engineer who traded his keyboard for a portafilter after tasting a single-origin Coorg espresso in a tiny roadside stall. That cup changed everything.

Every recipe at The Hearth is co-created with our head roaster, Deepa Krishnamurthy, who has spent a decade sourcing directly from indigenous farming communities across Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh, and Assam.

We are proud to be Bandra's first café to offer fully traceable, farm-to-cup sourcing with living wage certifications for every partner farm.
